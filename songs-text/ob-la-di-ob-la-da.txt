Notes: Album key is Bb. Form is V1>Ch>V2>Ch>Br>V3>Ch>Br>V4>Ch. The last time through on the chorus, play the 6m for the last chord (in place of the 1) to lead in to the outro. In the last verse, there is a cool arpeggio played on the piano in the 4th bar that is just a 1-3-5-3-1 run of the chord tones and then repeated again an octave up...it happens again in the 6th bar.
